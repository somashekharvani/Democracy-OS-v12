## **Top 5 "Tough" Questions & Answers**

### **Q1: How do you handle low internet in villages?**
**A:** "Democracy OS is designed with a 'Lite' mode and utilizes Homomorphic Encryption which allows for small data packets. We also support offline verification that syncs via blockchain once connectivity is restored."

### **Q2: Is blockchain actually necessary?**
**A:** "Absolutely. It provides an immutable, decentralized ledger that ensures no central authority—including us—can modify a single vote. It's about providing cryptographic proof of integrity."

### **Q3: How do you prevent identity theft?**
**A:** "We use a multi-factor approach: Voter ID verification + Facial recognition + Geolocation checking. Even if someone has your ID, they can't bypass the biometric and location shields."

### **Q4: Why focus on voice assistants?**
**A:** "In many emerging democracies, literacy is a barrier to digital participation. By providing a Voice Assistant in Hindi, Kannada, and other regional languages, we ensure democracy belongs to everyone, not just the tech-literate."

### **Q5: How does Homomorphic Encryption work here?**
**A:** "It allows the system to count votes while they are still encrypted. This means the tallying server never 'sees' individual choices, ensuring 100% privacy while maintaining 100% accuracy."
